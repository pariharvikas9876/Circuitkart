Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take? +
Delivery typically takes 1–3 days depending on your city. Ujjain and nearby areas get same-day or next-day delivery. Indore, Dewas, Agar and other cities take 2–3 days. We'll confirm your order and estimated delivery time via WhatsApp.
What is the delivery charge? +
A flat delivery charge of ₹50 applies on all orders. Additionally, if you choose Cash on Delivery, a ₹50 COD handling fee is also applicable. UPI payment has no extra charges beyond the delivery fee.
Can I get a bulk discount for college group orders? +
Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk and group orders. For example, the Line Follower Robot is ₹1000 each but order 3 at just ₹2700. For other products and larger quantities, contact us on WhatsApp to get a custom quote for your college club or group.
Do you deliver outside Ujjain and Indore? +
Yes! We deliver to Ujjain, Indore, Dewas, Agar, Gwalior, Bhopal, Ratlam, Mandsaur and many more cities across Madhya Pradesh. For deliveries outside these cities, please contact us on WhatsApp before placing the order.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI payment (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) and Cash on Delivery (COD). UPI payment is preferred as it is faster and has no extra charges. For COD orders, a ₹50 handling fee applies.
Are all components included in the kit? +
Yes! All our kits are complete — everything listed in the product components is included. You don't need to buy anything extra. We carefully pack and verify each kit before dispatch to ensure nothing is missing.
What if I receive a damaged or incomplete kit? +
Your satisfaction is our priority. If you receive a damaged or incomplete kit, contact us immediately on WhatsApp with photos. We will arrange a replacement or refund as quickly as possible. Please report any issues within 24 hours of delivery.
Can I visit the shop and pick up in person? +
Absolutely! You're welcome to visit our shop at 23/4 Dhaba Road, Ujjain, MP 456006. Shop hours are Mon–Sat 10AM–8PM and Sunday 11AM–6PM. We recommend calling ahead on +91 91795 30179 to confirm that your desired product is available before visiting.
